Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival
Brooklyn Bridge Park, 5th - 8th August 2018

About us

Basic information about Brooklyn O.F.F. 2018 event

  • The event is organized by Brooklyn Vibes Events Co.
  • 10 films will be showed at Brooklyn Bridge Park every evening from August 5th through 8th, from 6pm to midnight.
  • Full programme and movies are here.
  • Shows will be free and open to everyone but the capacity is 5000 seats for each evening.
  • There are only 5000 seats for each evening. Pre-register here for regular updates.
